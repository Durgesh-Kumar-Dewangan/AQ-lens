🚀 How to Use
  1. Click Show / Hide Lens above or the 🔍 button on any page
  2. Drag the blue lens over text you want to read
  3. It auto-reads after you stop dragging (toggle Auto-Read in the panel)
  4. Or press ▶ in the control panel below the lens
  5. Choose Normal (offline) or Meta/Instru-Read (Gemini AI)
